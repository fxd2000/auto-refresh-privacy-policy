Privacy Policy for Auto Refresh

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Data Collection

Auto Refresh does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

2. Data Storage

The only data stored are your extension preferences:

  • Refresh interval settings
  • Language preference
  • Tab refresh mode selection

This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • "activeTab": To refresh the current active tab
  • "tabs": To refresh multiple tabs when selected
  • "storage": To save your preferences locally

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics.

5. Data Security

All operations are performed locally within your browser. No data is transmitted over the internet.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted here and the "Last updated" date will be modified accordingly.
